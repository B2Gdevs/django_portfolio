About me

I am a 27 year old Marine veteran and software developer with the majority of skills invested in Python, Data Science, and Machine Learning/Deep Learning. I like looking at data and finding patterns and being up to date with new technologies. I have created Neural Networks in PyTorch and Keras, along with Genetic Algorithms to optimize their hyper-parameters. I have been moving in to web app development lately using Django and Bootstrap.

I received my Bachelors of Science majoring in Computer Science, minoring in Mathematics, in 2018 from Texas State University. During this time I worked as the Lead Virtual Reality Developer in one of the VR Labs at Texas State. I created a student organization called the Virtual Reality Development Group (VRDG) to help train and recruit students to continue VR development at Texas State.
